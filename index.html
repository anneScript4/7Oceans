What Matter

An exhibition exploring meaning over matter to enhance the perception of a digital space.

During the pandemic, weâ€™ve been increasingly introduced to digital spaces, and many of those spaces rely on perception we are used to in the physical world. Matter, is the defining perception of physical space. What we can touch, things we can see, the object we can hold on to.

But digital spaces are not defined by matter. We cannot touch a website, or hold a piece of code in the palm of our hand. Instead, what we rely the most on to percieve digital spaces is meaning, the story behind the matter and our relation to it. This project experiments with putting meaning above matter, and exploring how we can present photographs using more than a traditional flat image on a wall.
