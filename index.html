WHAT MATTER

exploring MEANING over MATTER to enhance the perception of a DIGITAL SPACE.

During the PANDEMIC,

weâ€™ve been increasingly introduced to digital spaces, and many of those spaces rely on perception we are used to in the physical world.

Matter,

is the defining perception of physical space. What we can touch, things we can see, the object we can hold on to.

But DIGITAL SPACES are not defined by MATTER

We cannot touch a website,

or hold a piece of code in the palm of our hand. Instead, what we rely the most on to percieve digital spaces is meaning, the story behind the matter and our relation to it.

This project experiments with putting MEANING ABOVE MATTER,

and EXPLORING,

how we can present photographs using more than a traditional flat image on a wall.

It's easy...

to share a physical space with someone, which is not the case for digital spaces. Because of the isolation and loneliness that the pandemic has caused, we wished for the project to encourage bridging the gap of connecting with a loved one online.

Affiliated with each object is a QR CODE,

that leads you to an augmented reality filter you can use on instagram.

We encourage you to INTERACT WITH THE INSTALLATION,

and to reach out to someone you miss, to bridge the social gap of digital spaces.
